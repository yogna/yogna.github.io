Who we are

Our company was established in 2000 and had been responsible for providing outstanding IT consulting services ever since. Our specialty is in the areas or onsite and offshore development, ERP implementation, custome solutions etc.

Yogna, Inc is a dynamic consulting services firm that provides system design and implementation services for the mission critical needs of manufacturing and distribution firms. We specialize in implementation of ERP packaged software, with a focus on Oracle's JD Edwards EnterpriseOne and JD Edwards World Software.

Yogna offers a unique combination of proven industry methods, and a tailored selection of experienced Functional and Technical experts to lead your organization through changes required to manage, build, and implement business driven solutions. We offer a flexible approach enabling you to select the scope of services that will meet your business goals.

At Yogna we deliver substantial business value on each and every client engagement. We do this by combining industry-specific business expertise and technical skills with proven project management methods and old school values.
